# Foracle Knowledge Base

Foracle's AI Assistant comes with built-in financial knowledge to help answer your questions. This page explains what topics the assistant knows about.

---

## What is the Knowledge Base?

The Knowledge Base is a collection of financial information that Foracle's AI Assistant can search through when you ask questions. Think of it as a "brain" of financial knowledge that helps the assistant give you accurate, helpful answers.

When you ask a general financial question, the assistant searches this knowledge base to find relevant information before responding.

---

## Topics Covered

### 1. CPF (Central Provident Fund)

Everything you need to know about Singapore's mandatory savings scheme:

- **What is CPF?** - An overview of the Central Provident Fund and its purpose
- **Contribution Rates** - How much employees and employers contribute (20% + 17% = 37% for those aged 55 and below)
- **Account Types**:
  - **Ordinary Account (OA)** - For housing, insurance, investment, education
  - **Special Account (SA)** - For retirement savings
  - **MediSave Account (MA)** - For healthcare expenses
- **Interest Rates** - How much each account earns (2.5% for OA, 4% for SA/MA)
- **Allocation Rates** - How contributions are split between the three accounts

**Example questions you can ask:**
- "What is CPF?"
- "How much CPF do I contribute?"
- "What are the CPF account types?"
- "What interest does my CPF earn?"

### 3. Budgeting Basics

Fundamental concepts to help you manage your money:

- **The 50/30/20 Rule** - A popular budgeting framework:
  - 50% for Needs (housing, food, transport)
  - 30% for Wants (entertainment, dining out)
  - 20% for Savings (emergency fund, retirement, investments)
- **Expense Categories**:
  - Fixed expenses (rent, insurance, subscriptions)
  - Variable expenses (groceries, utilities, transport)
  - Periodic expenses (annual fees, maintenance, gifts)
- **Budget Tracking Tips** - Best practices for staying on top of your finances

**Example questions you can ask:**
- "What is the 50/30/20 rule?"
- "What are fixed vs variable expenses?"
- "How should I track my budget?"
